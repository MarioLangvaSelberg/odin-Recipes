Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté garlic and onion in olive oil until soft.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
