Burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form ground beef into patties, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, adding cheese in the last minute if desired.
  4. Toast burger buns on the grill or skillet.
  5. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments.

Enjoy your delicious homemade burger!
