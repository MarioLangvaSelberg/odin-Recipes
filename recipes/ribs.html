Ribs

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. Mix brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the ribs.
  4. Place ribs on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil.
  5. Cover with another piece of foil and bake for 2.5 to 3 hours.
  6. Remove from oven, brush with barbecue sauce, and grill or broil for an additional 10-15 minutes until caramelized.

Enjoy your delicious homemade ribs!
